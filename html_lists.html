CHEESE SANDWICH RECIPE

List of ingredients:

  1. 2 slices of bread
  2. 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepperjack
  3. tspn of butter

Steps for preparation:

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Come with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
